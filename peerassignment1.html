What is mean total number of steps taken per day?

  1. Make a histogram of the total number of steps taken each day
  2. Calculate and report the mean and median total number of steps taken per day

I will use package plyr to count the necessary statistics and ggplot to plot the data. Histogram shows that 10000 steps has been the total number steps most often. The median number of steps is 10765, while mean is 10766
